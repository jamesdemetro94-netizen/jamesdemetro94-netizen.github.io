Emergency Roof Repair

What is emergency roof repair? Emergency roof repair is urgent work done when active water intrusion, storm damage, missing roof materials, or sudden roof failure threatens the inside of a home or building.

How much does emergency roof repair cost? Emergency roof repair often ranges from $450 to $2,500, depending on timing, access, roof type and the amount of temporary or permanent repair needed.

Example Emergency Repair Story

During heavy rain, a homeowner in Great Kills notices water dripping through a bedroom ceiling. A common repair in this situation may involve locating a failed flashing detail, sealing the leak area, replacing damaged shingles or pipe flashing, and checking nearby roof penetrations. A repair like this commonly falls between $600 and $1,100, depending on access and damage.

Quick Facts: Emergency roof repairs should be handled quickly. Even a small leak can damage insulation, drywall, framing and electrical areas if ignored.

Roof Leak Repair

What causes roof leaks? Roof leaks are commonly caused by failed flashing, missing shingles, cracked pipe boots, chimney leaks, skylight issues, open flat roof seams, ponding water, clogged gutters or storm damage.

How much does roof leak repair cost? Roof leak repair commonly ranges from $300 to $1,500 for localized leaks, but larger leaks with hidden damage can cost more.

Example Roof Leak Repair Story

A homeowner in New Dorp sees a brown ceiling stain after several days of rain. The roof inspection finds a cracked pipe boot and loose surrounding shingles. A typical repair may include replacing the pipe boot, sealing the surrounding flashing and securing nearby shingles. This type of repair often costs $350 to $750.

Asphalt Shingle Roof Repair

What is shingle roof repair? Shingle roof repair fixes missing, lifted, cracked, curled or storm-damaged shingles so the roof can shed water properly again.

How much does shingle repair cost? Most shingle repairs range from $350 to $1,200, depending on roof height, pitch, number of shingles and matching material.

Example Shingle Repair Story

After a windstorm in Tottenville, several ridge cap shingles and field shingles are lifted. A typical repair may include removing damaged shingles, installing matching replacements, sealing nail areas and checking nearby flashing. A repair like this often ranges from $500 to $1,400.

Roof Flashing Repair

What is roof flashing repair? Flashing repair fixes the metal or waterproofing details around chimneys, walls, skylights, valleys, vents and roof edges where leaks often begin.

How much does flashing repair cost? Roof flashing repair usually ranges from $400 to $1,500, depending on the detail and the amount of surrounding roof work needed.

Example Flashing Repair Story

In St. George, older homes often have roof transitions near chimneys and masonry walls. A common flashing repair may involve removing failed sealant, replacing step flashing, resealing counter flashing and checking shingles around the wall. Repairs like this commonly range from $700 to $2,000.

Flat Roof Repair

What is flat roof repair? Flat roof repair fixes leaks, open seams, punctures, ponding water issues, flashing problems and membrane damage on low-slope roofs.

How much does flat roof repair cost? Flat roof repairs often range from $500 to $3,500, depending on the roof system, leak source and repair area.

Example Flat Roof Repair Story

A small commercial building in Port Richmond has ponding water and an open membrane seam. A common repair may include cleaning the area, repairing the seam, reinforcing the membrane and sealing the leak path. Similar repairs often range from $900 to $2,400.

Repair is usually best for isolated damage. Replacement may be better for widespread deterioration, repeated leaks or an aging roof system.

A roof leak should be repaired as soon as possible because water can damage insulation, decking, drywall and framing.

Soffit & Fascia FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About Soffit & Fascia

What is soffit?

Soffit is the material installed underneath roof overhangs that helps protect the roof edge and may provide ventilation.

What is fascia?

Fascia is the board or material along the roof edge that helps protect the structure and often supports gutters.

Do you repair damaged soffits?

Yes. Maximum Roofing repairs cracked, damaged, loose, and deteriorated soffit systems.

Do you replace rotten fascia?

Yes. Damaged or rotted fascia boards can be repaired or replaced to restore roof edge protection.

Can soffits affect roof ventilation?

Yes. Ventilated soffits provide intake airflow that works with roof ventilation systems.

Why does fascia get damaged?

Common causes include moisture exposure, gutter problems, storms, and aging materials.

Do you repair storm-damaged soffit and fascia?

Yes. We repair exterior damage caused by wind, storms, and weather exposure.

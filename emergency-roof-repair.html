Emergency Repair vs Replacement

Can Emergency Roof Damage Be Repaired?

Many emergency roofing problems can be repaired when addressed quickly. Leaks, damaged flashing, missing shingles, and storm damage may only require targeted repairs.

If damage is extensive or the roofing system has reached the end of its useful life, Maximum Roofing can evaluate whether replacement is the better option.

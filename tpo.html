What Is TPO Roofing?

TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) roofing is a single-ply membrane roofing system designed for commercial flat and low-slope roofs. It creates a strong waterproof barrier that protects buildings from leaks, UV exposure, and harsh weather conditions.

Why TPO Roofing Is Better

20–30 Year Lifespan

A properly installed TPO roof can last approximately 20 to 30 years with proper maintenance.

Energy Efficient

The reflective surface helps reduce heat absorption and improves energy efficiency.

Waterproof Protection

Heat-welded seams create a strong barrier designed to help prevent leaks.

Weather Resistant

TPO roofing is designed to handle rain, snow, wind, UV exposure, and temperature changes.

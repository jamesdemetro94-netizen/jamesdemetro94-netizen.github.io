Damaged, loose, or failing chimney flashing can be repaired to restore water protection around the chimney.

Step Flashing

Counter Flashing

Installation Process

How Chimney Flashing Is Installed

1. Roof Inspection

The chimney area, roof materials, flashing, and possible leak sources are evaluated.

2. Remove Damaged Materials

Old or failing flashing components are removed when necessary.

3. Prepare Chimney Area

The roof and chimney surfaces are prepared for proper flashing installation.

4. Install Flashing

Step flashing, counter flashing, and waterproofing details are installed.

5. Final Inspection

The completed flashing system is checked for proper fit and water protection.

Chimney Leak Problems

Common Chimney Flashing Problems We Repair

Leaking Chimney Flashing

Damaged or failing flashing can allow water to enter where the chimney meets the roof.

Damaged Step Flashing

Step flashing problems can create leaks along chimney sides and roof intersections.

Loose Counter Flashing

Loose or damaged counter flashing can allow moisture behind the chimney area.

Storm Damage

Wind, heavy rain, and weather exposure can damage chimney flashing systems.

Aging Flashing Materials

Older flashing may deteriorate and require repair or replacement.

Roof-To-Chimney Leaks

The connection between roofing and chimneys requires proper waterproofing.

Chimney Flashing FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About Chimney Flashing

What is chimney flashing?

Chimney flashing is a waterproofing system that seals the connection between a chimney and the roof to help prevent leaks.

Do you repair chimney flashing leaks?

Yes. Maximum Roofing repairs chimney flashing leaks caused by damaged flashing, weather damage, and roof connection problems.

What causes chimney leaks?

Common causes include damaged flashing, failed seals, aging materials, storms, and improper installation.

Can chimney flashing be replaced?

Yes. Old or damaged flashing systems can be replaced with properly installed new flashing components.

Do you repair step flashing?

Yes. Step flashing repairs help protect the areas where the chimney meets the roof surface.

Why is chimney flashing important?

Proper chimney flashing helps prevent water intrusion and protects the roof, chimney, and interior areas of the property.

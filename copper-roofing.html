Installation Process

How Copper Roofing Is Installed

1. Roof Inspection

The roof structure, decking, slope, ventilation, and existing conditions are evaluated.

2. Preparation

The roof surface is prepared and damaged materials are addressed before copper installation.

3. Underlayment & Flashing

Protective layers and detailed flashing are installed to help prevent water intrusion.

4. Copper Installation

Copper panels, sheets, or roofing components are installed with proper techniques.

5. Final Inspection

Seams, edges, flashing, drainage, and completed roofing details are reviewed.

Copper Roof Repair

Common Copper Roofing Problems We Repair

Copper Roof Leaks

Leaks can develop from seams, flashing failures, roof penetrations, damaged sections, or improper installation details.

Damaged Copper Panels

Bent, damaged, or worn copper sections may require repair to maintain roof performance.

Copper Flashing Issues

Damaged flashing around chimneys, walls, skylights, and valleys can allow water intrusion.

Storm Damage Repair

Wind, falling branches, and severe weather can affect copper roofing components.

Seam & Joint Repairs

Copper seams and connections require proper attention to maintain waterproofing.

Drainage Problems

Poor drainage, clogged gutters, or roof design issues can contribute to moisture problems.

Copper Roof Maintenance

Protecting Your Copper Roof

  • Schedule regular copper roof inspections.
  • Keep gutters and drainage systems clear.
  • Inspect flashing and roof transitions.
  • Remove damaging debris buildup.
  • Monitor seams and connections.
  • Address leaks quickly.
  • Maintain proper attic ventilation.
Repair vs Replacement

Should You Repair or Replace a Copper Roof?

Small issues such as leaks, damaged flashing, or isolated damaged sections can often be repaired. Extensive damage or aging systems may require replacement.

Maximum Roofing evaluates the copper panels, seams, flashing, drainage, underlayment, and overall roof condition before recommending the best solution.

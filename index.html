What should I do when my roof starts leaking?

Move belongings away from the affected area, contain interior water when safe, and call a roofing contractor. Avoid climbing onto a wet or storm-damaged roof.

Yes. We can inspect for visible hail-related damage to roofing materials and related components. The presence of damage and insurance coverage are separate determinations.

Do you replace roofs in Staten Island?

How do I know whether I need repair or replacement?

The decision depends on roof age, the extent and location of damage, recurring leaks, material condition, decking, drainage, previous repairs, and the expected value of another repair.

How much does roof repair cost in Staten Island?

How much does a new roof cost?

How often should a roof be inspected?

A roof should generally be checked periodically and after severe weather. Older roofs, flat roofs, and roofs with previous leaks may benefit from more frequent inspection.

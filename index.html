Roof Decision Guide

Flat Roof Repair vs Replacement

Deciding between repairing or replacing a flat roof depends on the age of the roofing system, amount of damage, moisture conditions, and overall roof performance. A professional inspection helps determine the best solution.

When Repair Makes Sense

Flat roof repairs are usually recommended for isolated problems such as leaks, damaged seams, flashing failures, punctures, or small membrane issues when the roof system is still performing.

When Replacement Is Needed

Replacement may be needed when the roof has widespread deterioration, wet insulation, repeated leaks, structural concerns, or the repair costs continue increasing.

Professional Roof Evaluation

Maximum Roofing evaluates the membrane, seams, flashing, drainage, insulation, previous repairs, and overall roof condition before recommending repair or replacement.

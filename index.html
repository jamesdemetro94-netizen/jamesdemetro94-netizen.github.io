Roofing Information for Staten Island Property Owners

When Should You Repair or Replace a Roof?

Roof repairs are usually best for isolated problems such as leaks, damaged flashing, or small areas of damage. Roof replacement may be needed when the system has widespread deterioration or repeated failures.

What Causes Roof Leaks?

Common causes include damaged roofing materials, failed seams, flashing problems, poor drainage, storm damage, and aging roofing systems.

How Long Does a Roof Last?

Roof lifespan depends on materials, installation quality, weather exposure, ventilation, and regular maintenance.
